Description

Cacio e pepe is a typical Lazio's pasta dish that needs 3 ingredients and it's very quick to make. You can think of it as the Gricia without guanciale. The sauce is basically a cream of hot pecorino cheese and pepper (as the name suggests). It can be tricky at first but with a few tricks and the right timing, you can really make a creamy and tasty meal in just 20 minutes.

Let's start!

Ingredients

Quantities listed here are for 1 person, just multiply everything for the number of people you're going to cook for.

  • 80 g of spaghetti
  • 50 g of pecorino romano cheese
  • 0.5 spoons of black pepper
