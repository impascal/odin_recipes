Description

Amatriciana pasta is a tasty variation on the classic tomato pasta. It's a notorious dish from Rome, which adds Roman Pecorino cheese (sheep cheese) and Guanciale (pork cheek) as base ingredients.

It's probably the most straightforward of the “Big 4s” of Roman pasta recipes, or at least definitely easier than Carbonara or Cacio e Pepe to make for the unexperienced.
